3D Commerce

Replacing $180K/Year in Product Photography with a 3D Pipeline

Result: 67% cost reduction. Infinite SKU variations. Zero studio time.

A custom cabinetry brand was spending $180K annually on physical photoshoots for their 400+ SKU catalog. We built a PBR rendering pipeline and a browser-based WebGL configurator that generates every possible combination in real-time. The photoshoot budget was reallocated to paid media.

Engagement Type

Audit ($5K) → Build ($85K)

RevOps

The $500K Conversation: Whale Curve Analysis Exposes 40 Underwater Dealers

Result: $430K recovered annually by enforcing minimum order thresholds.

A national building products manufacturer knew "some dealers weren't worth it" but couldn't prove it. We ran an Activity-Based Costing workshop, plotted the Whale Curve, and delivered a dealer scorecard that definitively exposed 40 accounts where cost-to-serve exceeded revenue. Minimum thresholds were enforced. Annual savings: $430K.

Engagement Type

Audit ($10K) → Retainer ($12K/mo)
